iTest Environmental, LLC, (“iTEST”), and CLIENT named above agree as follows:

1.

iTEST shall provide assessment and testing services at the above described property address, as a subcontractor overseen by Expert Network, Inc. a project management and logistics company. Client and iTEST agree that any and all testing and assessment services being provided are being performed on a non-emergency basis and not to address or prevent any potential damage, as iTEST is not a remediation company. An estimate of services is being provided to Client and is hereby incorporated herein as part of this agreement by reference, and Client agrees and understands that Expert Network, Inc. shall be directly entitled to its standard ten percent (10%) overhead and ten percent (10%) profit in addition to any final invoice as overseeing general building contractor.

2.

Client shall provide access to the property to iTEST for the above services to be performed, and agrees to cooperate with iTEST to ensure that payments are made by any insurance carrier immediately upon completion of work. Client understands that iTEST is working for Client and not Client’s insurance carrier, and that Client is ultimately responsible for payment for any services provided, and that the obligation to pay for those services arises directly out of this contract.

3. Direction to Pay.

Client hereby demands and authorizes any applicable insurance carrier(s) to pay iTEST solely and directly for any and all services provided, while Expert Network, Inc. shall be directly entitled to be paid its overhead and profit. Client or any co-insured should not be included as a payee. Client directs the insurance carrier to take any applicable deductible from other coverages or payments being made to or claimed by Client and not from any payments being made to iTEST. If any form of testing done is not covered or allowed under Client’s policy of insurance, Client hereby directs Client’s insurance carrier(s) to issue payment for same directly to iTEST and to deduct any such payments from payments being made for covered portions of Client’s loss.

4. Letter of Protection.

Client hereby authorizes and instructs any agent, representative or attorney who now or in the future represents Client on any claim against Client’s insurance carrier related to this loss to issue to iTEST a letter of protection for the full amount of any and all services provided, and directs any such agent, representative or attorney to pay iTEST out of any funds received for the claim or any settlement related to the claim. Client further agrees to inform any attorney or representative of iTEST’s contract and invoice.

Your use of such websites will be subject to the terms of use applicable to each such website.

5. Consent to Communicate.

Client hereby directs my insurance carrier(s) and mortgage provider to communicate directly with iTEST and release any and all information requested by it, its representative, and/or its attorney for the direct purpose of obtaining actual benefits to be paid for services rendered or to be rendered. In this regard Client waives Client’s privacy rights. Should no claim yet exist, Client agrees and authorizes that submission of this document shall operate as a first notice of a claim to Client’s insurance carrier(s) on Client’s behalf.

6. Request to Exceed Cap.

Client understands that Client’s insurance policy may contain an arbitrary cap on services which requires additional approval. Should such a cap be contained in Client’s policy, submission of this document hereby operates as a direct request to Client’s insurance carrier for approval to exceed such cap.

All fees, costs and charges reflected on an Invoice shall be due within fifteen (15) days after the date of the Invoice. For any outstanding balance not paid in full within thirty (30) days from the date of the Invoice, interest shall accrue on the unpaid balance at the rate of 1.5% per month (18% per annum) from the date of default.

8. Assignment of Insurance Claim Benefits.

If Client’s insurance policy does not contain a total restriction prohibiting the assignment of post-loss claim benefits, Client hereby assigns to iTEST any and all insurance rights, benefits, and proceeds which pertain to services rendered in relation to the above loss, under any applicable policy of insurance, whether listed on this contract or not. This assignment is limited to the amount of iTEST’s invoice for services rendered in relation to the above claim and the right and ability to collect same directly from my insurer, including the right to file suit and to seek attorney's fees and court costs. To the extent iTEST may perform multiple types of services or services which may fall under different coverage portions of Client’s policy of insurance, Client hereby assigns to iTEST a separate assignment of post-loss claim benefits pertaining to each category, coverage section of the policy, and invoice for services. Client hereby waves any homestead exemption which might be applicable to such insurance funds. Any and all other insurance rights, benefits, and proceeds shall continue to belong to the Client. If the assignment of insurance claim benefits is applicable, iTEST shall provide a copy of this agreement to Client’s insurance carrier within 3 business days after the date on which the assignment agreement is executed, or on the date which work begins, whichever is earlier.
Client/Assignor understands that the assignment of benefits portion of this contract, if applicable, may be rescinded without penalty or fee by submitting a written notice of rescission signed by Client/Assignor to iTEST within 14 days after the execution of the agreement, at least 30 days after the date work on the property is scheduled to commence if the iTEST has not substantially performed, or at least 30 days after the execution of the agreement if the agreement does not contain a commencement date and the assignee has not begun substantial work on the property. Any such notice must be sent via certified mail to EXPERT NETWORK at 14411 Commerce Way, Suite 300, Miami Lakes, FL 33016 or via e-mail delivery to collections@itestenvironmental.com. The Notice will be deemed to be submitted on the date it is received by EXPERT NETWORK.
In the event of a rescission of the assignment of benefits, Client is then obligated for direct and immediate payment of any contracted work performed before the assignment of benefits is rescinded. The obligation to pay for such services arises out of this work authorization and not from the assignment itself. In the event of a rescission of the assignment of benefits, or if Client’s insurance policy contains a total restriction prohibiting the assignment of post-loss claim benefits, then this term shall not apply and the parties agree that this contract shall construed and, if necessary, reformed as though this term was not contained herein.

9. Dispute Between the Parties.

The Parties hereby agree that if there is any dispute between them, jurisdiction for such action will lie in Broward County, Florida. Additionally, if iTEST must bring legal action to recover any amount it is owed, it shall be entitled to recover the cost of collection, including all attorney’s fees and court costs.

Client hereby appoints iTEST as Client’s attorney in fact to endorse and deposit any payments made by any source for services rendered by iTEST which may include Client’s name as a co-payee.
